recent news: 
january 2000: skam records launched their official website at http://www.skam.co.uk/   

december 1999: boc realaudio clips now online on the sounds page.   
   
november 1999: boc appeared live with autechre and squarepusher at the warp records 10th birthday party in london on november 5, with DJing support from mira calix and aphex twin.    
   
october 1999: as part of warp's 10th birthday celebrations, the label released '10+3 remixes', a compilation album featuring warp artists remixed by various artists.   
boards of canada's 'an eagle in your mind' is remixed by push button objects, and the unreleased track 'kid for today' is remixed by stereolab. see records for more details.   

september 1999: skam records re-released boards of canada's 1996 EP 'hi scores' on vinyl & CD. (cat. number SKA008).
